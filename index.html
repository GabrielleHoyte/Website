About me

Born and raised in Boston, MA to Trinidadian immigrants, I was always interested in the arts. Music, art and fashion were very important to my parents and they quickly became important to me as well. I pursued a computer animation degree from Full Sail Univerity and worked to establish myself as a digital artist. More recently however, I decided to shift my career path and finally venture into software engineering to complement my artistic background. As a full-stack engineer, I work with programming such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript and I'm always looking for new and exciting coding languages to add to my repertoire

When I'm not coding or drawing, I'm traveling to new countries, playing video games, bingeing the latest shows or pounding the pavement with friends to try restaurants that I've never been to.
